Building Process

Construction Phases

Understanding the stages of your construction project from planning to completion

01

Planning & Design

  • Site analysis and feasibility studies
  • Architectural design development
  • Engineering and systems planning
  • Budget estimation and scheduling
2-4 weeks
02

Permits & Approvals

  • Building permit applications
  • Regulatory compliance review
  • Environmental assessments
  • Documentation preparation
3-6 weeks
03

Site Preparation

  • Land clearing and excavation
  • Foundation preparation
  • Utility connections setup
  • Temporary facilities installation
2-3 weeks
04

Foundation & Framing

  • Concrete foundation pouring
  • Structural framing construction
  • Roof and exterior wall assembly
  • Quality inspections
4-8 weeks
05

Mechanical Systems

  • Electrical wiring installation
  • Plumbing and HVAC systems
  • Smart technology integration
  • Safety systems setup
3-6 weeks
06

Interior Finishing

  • Drywall and insulation
  • Flooring and tiling installation
  • Painting and decorative finishes
  • Cabinetry and fixtures
4-8 weeks
07

Final Inspections

  • Quality control assessments
  • Building code compliance verification
  • System functionality testing
  • Final walkthrough with client
1-2 weeks
08

Project Completion

  • Final documentation delivery
  • Certificate of occupancy obtainment
  • Client training on systems
  • Warranty and maintenance planning
1 week

Please note that the duration of each phase can vary depending on project size, complexity, and other factors. Our team will provide you with a detailed timeline specific to your project during the consultation process.
